Saiga Antelope

Samal

A prominent feature of the saiga is the pair of closely spaced, bloated nostrils directed downward.

  • Scientific Name: Saiga tatarica
  • Average Length: 100 ~ 140 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 6~12 years
  • Habitat: Eurasian Steppe

The saiga stands 61–81 cm at the shoulder, and weighs 26–69 kg.During summer migrations, a saiga's nose helps filter out dust kicked up by the herd and cools the animal's blood. In the winter, it heats up the frigid air before it is taken to the lungs.
